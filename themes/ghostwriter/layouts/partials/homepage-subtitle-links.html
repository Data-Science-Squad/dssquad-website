➤ WHAT IS THE DSSQUAD CRIME PREDICTOR

The DSSQUAD crime predictor is a software product built to predict the short-term occurrences of crime in different parts of Buffalo city, US.

The crime predictor was developed out of the need to curb the numerous occurrences of crime in the Buffalo city, US.
